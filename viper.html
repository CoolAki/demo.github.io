Vipers abilities explained



Ability 1(snake pit)-This ability is a toxin molly and is similar to brims and can bounce of walls but gets activated if it hits the ground,u can use this also for afterplant.But even u and ur teamates get damaged from this pit.



Ability 2(poison cloud)-This ability is a toxin grenade that can be set in places and when u press the q key a toxin smoke gets activated.It can be used to smoke out an area so that enemies don't shoot u from that area.The enemies who come inside this smoke get a 50 health decay for some time.The enemies are also vulnerable so u can kill them easily.



Ability 3(toxin screen)-This ability is a toxin wall that can be used to cover an area or create cover for urself.If the enemies go through ur wall they recieve a 50 health decay.



Ability 4(vipers pit)-This ability is basically a toxin gas spread about thee size of a site and if the enemy enters this gas he will be highlighted in red so u can see the enemies easily and also they recieve a decay so it is easy for u to kill them.Ur teamates do not get decayed.If u leave the gas there will be a bar on ur screen that shows how much time the gas will be there for if u don't go and refuel it by staying inside.
