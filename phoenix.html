Phoenix abilities explained



Ability 1(hot hands)-This ability is a molly which can be used to heal urself or damage the enemies.It can be thrown to a certain height.If u throw it above the height it get bounces back to the ground.Its very good for checking corners without going there as u can use the molly for making the enemy come out.



Ability 2(blaze)-This ability is a fire wall which can be used to block a certain area to give u a clear entry.Enemies and even teamates can walk through it but they get damaged and the wall is indestructible and enemies cannot see anything through the wall.



Ability 3(curveball)-This ability is a curved flash bang that flashes enemies,It can be used around corners and obstacles.U can curve the flash left by pressing left click and curve the flash right by pressing right click.



Ultimate(run it back)-This ability is a marker placed at the location u activate ur ult,when u use ur ult u are in a state that when u die u will respawn at ur marker with full hp but no shields.There will be a bar on ur screen that denotes the time limit u have in ur ult state before u go back to ur marker
