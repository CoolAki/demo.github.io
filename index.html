Valorant

Valorant is a tactical agent based 5 vs 5 shooting game where there are two sides,the attackers and the defenders.The attackers have to plant the spike while the defenders have to defuse it.This game has many agents.This game requires everybody in your team to be useful and co-ordinate with ur team to win the game.When u win 13 rounds u win the game.After 12 rounds u switch sides.The defenders become attackers and vice versa.This website is for new players in valorant,this will teach you about all the abilities of the characters and more tips and tricks,click on the agents picture for more info about the agent.
