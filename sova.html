Sovas abilities explained



Ability 1(owl drone)-This ability is a drone as the name shows and can be used to gather intel and u can shoot intel darts that tags the enemies.U can increase the height of the drone but only up to a certain limit.There is a short amount of time until the drone gets destroyed.



Ability 2(shock bolt)-This ability is a shock dart that damages enemies for 80 damage.U can choose the power of the dart by pressing left click and u can choose the amount of times u want it to bounce by pressing right click.



Ability 3(recon bolt)-This ability is a intel dart that has the same bounce and charge system like shock dart.Anybody who is in the range of that dart gets tagged.



Ability 4(hunters fury)-This ability is energy blast released from a bow and it has 3 charges.Anybody who gets hit from the ult gets tagged.1 hit deals 80 damage.
