Kj abilities explained



Ability 1(nanoswarm)-This ability is a device that releases a swarm that damages enemies and even u and ur teamates.U can throw this anywhere u want.Its like vipers toxin except u have to press a button to release it.Anybody in the swarms radius gets damaged.U can use this to prevent the enemy from defusing.It has 2 charges.The duration of the swarm is only a little bit.It can be destroyed.It is invisble for ur enemies unless they come at a certain distance from the swarm.This is the same for killjoys alarmbot.



Ability 2(alarmbot)-This ability is a bot that can be put anywhere.Anybody who comes in it radius gets tracked by the bot and also becomes vulnerable.It also can be taken back.But it takes 15 seconds for it to recharge.It gets disabled once u get 40m away from the alarmbot.This is the same for her turret.



Ability 3(turret)-This ability is simply a turret as it name shows.equip a turret. Fire to deploy a turret that automatically fires at enemies in a 180 degree cone. hold equip to recall the deployed turret. Deactivates if Killjoy is more than 40m away from them. Re-entering the 40m range reactivates it.



Ultimate(lockdown)-This ability is a device that detains people in its radius.U can see the radius on the map.There is a time limit before the enemies who are in the radius get detained.This device can be destroyed by the enemies.The players who are detained cannot run,shoot and use abilities.U can use this to retake sites and get people out of the site so that u can defuse easily.
